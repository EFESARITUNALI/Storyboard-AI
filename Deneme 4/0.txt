Lumina is a bustling metropolis renowned for its towering skyscrapers and neon-lit nights. However, an unexpected blackout suddenly plunges the city into darkness. In the shadows of an alleyway, we see a figure shrouded in a black cloak, their face hidden by a mask, representing the notorious thief, Shadow. The silhouette of a person, later revealed to be Aria, is visible in the distance, standing in front of a towering skyscraper, her face determined and illuminated by the light emitting from her suit.
